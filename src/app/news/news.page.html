Chelsea have been crowned champions of Europe for the second time in their history, after defeating Manchester City 1-0 in the Champions League final. The Blues put in a solid defensive performance, with Kai Havertz scoring the only goal of the game.

 England Reach Euro 2020 Final

It's coming home! England have reached the final of Euro 2020 after a 2-1 win over Denmark in the semi-finals. The Three Lions will face Italy in the final, and will be hoping to win their first major tournament since the 1966 World Cup.
